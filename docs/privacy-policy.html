Privacy Policy for Event Multiselect for Google Calendar

Last updated: 6 April 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Event Multiselect for Google Calendar browser extension ("the Extension") that was created by Faith Lee Kye Yen ("the developer") as an Open Source service.

1. Data Collected

The Extension requires you to manually input the following information:

  • Calendar names
  • Calendar IDs

2. Data Storage

The calendar names and IDs you provide are:

  • Stored locally in your browser using Chrome's chrome.storage API.
  • Not transmitted, shared, or stored on any external server.
  • Not accessible to the developer or any third party.

3. Data Usage

  • The calendar ID is used to retrieve and edit events via the Google Calendar API, under the scope https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events.
  • The calendar name is used only for display and organizational purposes within the Extension.
  • The Extension does not collect, track, or log your events or any other calendar content.

4. Permissions and OAuth

To function properly, the Extension requests your authorization via Google OAuth. You must explicitly grant permission for the calendar.events scope, which allows the Extension to manage your calendar events.

You can revoke these permissions at any time via your Google Account permissions.

5. Third Parties

The Extension does not share data with any third parties. All operations are performed locally in your browser and through direct interaction with Google's official APIs.

6. Your Control

You have full control over the data entered and stored by the Extension. You can:

  • Edit or remove calendar names/IDs within the Extension UI
  • Clear your browser's storage/cache
  • Remove the Extension from Chrome at any time
  • Revoke API access via your Google Account permissions

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected here, with the "Last updated" date revised accordingly.
